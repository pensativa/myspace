Legal Services

Legal

Any buying procedure should be made with guidance of lawyer.

The buying or investing procedure is usually guided by lawyer, who will check and insure there are no loans on the property and will make a clear procedure of the deal until issuing the title.

Cyprus Land Registry Office undertakes transfer of ownership from seller to buyer. Immovable property can be bought by EU citizens and foreigners in Cyprus without any permissions.

Article 23 in the Constitution of Cyprus is the right of ownership of immovable property which empowers non-Cypriots and permanent citizens who own a property on the island to have the same property rights, you can be confident that your property is completely protected by law.

Our professionals in legal sphere will always be willing to solve your legal issues when possible.
